Yanan (Yaya) Guo

Hi there! I am currently a third-year PhD student at the University of Pittsburgh, working with my advisor Dr. Jun Yang in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. I graduated from Xidian University in 2018 with a B.S. in Telecommunications Engineering.

My research interests lie in the areas of computer architecture and security, with a focus on memory attacks and defenses, ML attacks and defenses, and quantum computers.
